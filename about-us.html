Our Story

Founded in 2024, MayaPets started with a simple observation: finding high-quality, trusted pet nutrition and healthcare in the Tamil market shouldn't be difficult.

We've grown from a small family initiative into a comprehensive platform that combines premium products, professional pharmacy services, and expert grooming advice — all delivered with love.

Our commitment remains the same: treat every pet as if they were our own. That's why we only source brands we trust and products we'd give to our own pets.
